Superiorni Atleta Management System
by Green Flamingos
Project Description
A service for managing training sessions and subscriptions for individuals or groups of trainees. It is a tailored Content Management system that enables the coach to track payments, schedule sessions, track the progress of clients and manage website content such as blog posts, gallery etc. Users get their own dashboard where they can statistically track both their progress and the progress of other gym members. The result is the creation of a small social app for trainees. The service also has a custom built analytics tool that tracks page clicks and represents it visually through daily, weekly, monthly and annual reports.


Technologies used:
  • Java EE
  • Thymeleaf
  • HTML/CSS
  • Javascript
  • Tomcat
  • CentOS linux distribution
  • Instagram Api
